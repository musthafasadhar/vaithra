Verification Criteria

What It Takes to Get Verified

1

Identity Verified

Real name, real LinkedIn profile, and a valid WhatsApp number. No anonymous profiles in the verified tier. You must be who you say you are.

2

Build Verified

You must have at least one shipped product, live prototype, or documented MVP. Show us something real — a link, a GitHub repo, screenshots, or user feedback.

3

Community Standing

Active participation in the Vaithra community — attending events, contributing to discussions, or having a city representative vouch for your seriousness.

+

PoB Score (Bonus)

Having at least one peer-validated Proof of Build milestone gives your application significantly more weight. Not required, but strongly recommended.

+

Traction Evidence (Bonus)

Users, revenue, letters of intent, or any other evidence that your idea has demand in the real world. Even 10 real users counts.

✕

What It Is Not

The badge is not for people who are "planning to build." It is for people who have already started, are currently building, and can prove it.
