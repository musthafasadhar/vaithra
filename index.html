The Foundation

India's next great founders aren't just in Bengaluru or Mumbai. They're everywhere but — isolated, talented, invisible.

Vaithra is a founder discovery and validation network operating city-by-city across India. We identify serious builders outside traditional startup hubs, organize local meetups, and create structured pathways for collaboration and visibility.

Built by founders for founders. Governed by a clear protocol. Powered by a community that values execution over noise.
